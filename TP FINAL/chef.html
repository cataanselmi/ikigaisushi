Our Chef

Born in 1947 in Saitama (Japan), Nobuyuki Matsuhisa is one of the most important chefs in the world. So much so that one of its well-known restaurants, the Matsuhisa in Beverly Hills, is among the most expensive in the world, where the "tasting menu" costs around $ 300 per diner. His father, an inveterate traveler, knew how to transmit to his son the taste for knowing new cities and their gastronomy before dying in a motorcycle accident when Nobu was 7 years old.
